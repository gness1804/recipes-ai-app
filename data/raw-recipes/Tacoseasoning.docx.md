Taco seasoning

Rating: 7/10

One whole batch (for tofu tacos): (halve this for the 2 smaller boxes of 8 oz each)  
1 tbsp chili powder (half: 1.5 tsp)  
1/2 tsp garlic powder (half: 1/4 tsp)  
1/2 tsp onion powder (half: 1/4 tsp)  
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (half: 1/4 tsp)  
1/2 tsp dried oregano (half: 1/4 tsp)  
1 tsp paprika (half: 1/2 tsp)  
3 tsp cumin (half: 1.5 tsp)  
2 tsp sea/kosher salt (half: 1 tsp)  
2 tsp black pepper (half: 1 tsp)  
(other variation)  
For each bean:  
1 tsp chili powder  
1/8 tsp garlic powder  
1/8 tsp onion powder  
1/8 tsp red pepper flakes  
1/8 tsp dried oregano  
1/4 tsp paprika  
3/4 tsp cumin  
1/2 tsp sea/kosher salt  
1/2 tsp black pepper  
Mix all ingredients in bowl.
