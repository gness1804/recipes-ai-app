**Ingredients:** 

1. Potatoes (2-3)   
2. Fragrant veggies (cilantro, scallions, basil, etc.)  
3. Olive oil  
4. Water  
5. Salt, pepper, and maybe other spices

**Procedure:**

1. Peel potatoes.  
2. Cut potatoes into small evenly cut strips (1/3 to 1/2 inch strips lengthwise) x 3 cuts on the side))  
3. Cut up scallions, basil, or other *fragrant* green veggies into small pieces. (Substitute yellow/white onion for scallions if scallions are not available).   
4. Heat oil in a pan that has a lid.  
5. Put potatoes in hot oil; season potatoes with pepper, salt, and maybe other spices.   
6. Add water (only enough to ensure potatoes do not burn)  
7. Cook potatoes in oil for \~3-5 min. \*\*put and keep lid on during cooking\!  
8. When cooking is about halfway done, add chopped fragrant greens. \*\*leave greens on top of potatoes; do not stir.  
9. When greens have wilted, mix them with potatoes. Again, keep the lid on as much as possible.  
10. Take out of the pan and serve.
