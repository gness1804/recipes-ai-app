Roast Potatoes

http://allrecipes.com/Recipe/Roast-Potatoes/Detail.aspx?event8=1\&prop24=SR\_Title\&e11=potato\&e8=Quick%20Search\&event10=1\&e7=Home%20Page\&soid=sr\_results\_p1i9

2 pounds red potatoes, cut into quarters  
2 tablespoons vegetable oil  
1 teaspoon salt (\*use less)  
1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper  
1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed  
Total: 900 cal, 17g protein, 32g fat, 2300 mg sodium

Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (250 degrees C).

Place potatoes in a large roasting pan and toss with oil, salt, pepper, and rosemary until evenly coated. Spread out potatoes in a single layer.

Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve immediately. (about 45 min at 450 degrees for 2 lb)
