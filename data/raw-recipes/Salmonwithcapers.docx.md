Salmon with capers

1 lb salmon (440 cal, 92g protein, 10g fat, 540mg sodium)  
1 tablespoon capers (255mg sodium)  
1 lemon (juice)  
Dijon mustard (to taste)  
1 cup quinoa (480 cal, 18g protein, 7.5g fat, 30mg sodium)  
Total: 920 cal, 110g protein, 17.5g fat, 825g sodium)

Defrost salmon.

Place salmon in baking dish.

Cover salmon with: the capers, lemon juice, and Dijon mustard (used sparingly)(cook fish about 15 min @ 450\)

Cook quinoa according to package directions; serve under salmon.
