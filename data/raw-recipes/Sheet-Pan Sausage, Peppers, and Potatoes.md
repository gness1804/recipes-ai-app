### **Sheet-Pan Sausage, Peppers, and Potatoes**

Feeds 3–4, 35 minutes (mostly hands-off)

Verdict: did not turn out well; isn't substantial enough. At the minimum, it needs to be accompanied by something substantial, such as a hearty salad or maybe rice. 

Ingredients

* 1 lb smoked sausage or Italian sausage (cut into rounds)

* 1 lb small potatoes, quartered

* 2 bell peppers, sliced

* 1 red onion, sliced

* 3 tbsp olive oil

* 1 tsp smoked paprika \+ ½ tsp oregano

* Salt and pepper

Steps

1. Heat oven to 425°F.

2. Toss potatoes, peppers, onion, and sausage with oil, paprika, oregano, salt, pepper.

3. Spread on a sheet pan. Roast 25–30 minutes, stirring once, until potatoes are crisp and everything’s browned.

4. Serve with mustard or crusty bread.
