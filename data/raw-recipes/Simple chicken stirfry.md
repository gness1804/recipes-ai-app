Simple chicken stir fry  
My original recipe 

Rating: 7/10

Ingredients:

1. 1 lb chicken thighs   
2. 1-2 cups rice  
3. Green bell pepper   
4. Red bell pepper   
5. 1-2 tablespoons of teriyaki sauce.  
6. 1 teaspoon ginger  
7. Sesame seeds (quantity up to the cook)  
8. (Optional) other spices.

Directions:

1. Cut up the chicken.  
2. Cut up the bell peppers.  
3. Make rice on the side.  
4. Cook chicken in a pan.  
5. Stir in cut up bell peppers.  
6. Add the teriyaki sauce   
7. Add the ginger and other spices.  
8. Serve over rice.
