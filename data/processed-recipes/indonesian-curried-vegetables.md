# Indonesian Curried Vegetables

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 2.25 cups water
- 2.5 cups quick-cooking brown rice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 cup broccoli florets, already cut
- 1 cup fresh mushrooms, already sliced
- 1 medium green bell pepper, cut into strips
- 1 cup red cabbage, already shredded
- 1 cup carrots, already shredded
- 2 cups onions, frozen and chopped
- 1 tablespoon curry powder, good quality imported
- 2 teaspoons garlic, bottled minced
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ginger, bottled chopped
- 2 cans (14.5 oz. ea.) diced tomatoes
- 3 tablespoons peanut butter

## Instructions

1. Bring 2.25 cups of water to a boil in a 2-quart saucepan. Add the rice, cover the pan, reduce the heat to low, and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from the heat and let stand, covered, until ready to serve.
2. Heat the oil in a 4.5-quart Dutch oven or soup pot over medium-high heat. Add the broccoli, mushrooms, and bell pepper, and cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes.
3. Add the red cabbage, carrots, and onions. Stir in the curry powder, garlic, cumin, and ginger. Stir in the tomatoes and their juice. Raise the heat to high and bring the stew to a boil. Uncover the pot and boil, stirring from time to time, for 3 minutes more to blend flavors.
4. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the peanut butter, blending well. Serve over the brown rice.
