# Renee's spinach curry

Rating: N

## Ingredients

- 1 can peas
- 2 cans coconut milk
- 1 can tomato paste
- 7 green peppers, diced
- 5 onions, diced
- 2 large potatoes (about 1 lb. each), diced
- 1 lb. shrimp
- 1 bag frozen chopped spinach
- 3 teaspoons curry
- 0.5 teaspoon cayenne
- 2 pinches black pepper
- 1 medium bay leaf
- 1.5 teaspoons garam masala
- 0.5 teaspoon turmeric
- 0.5 teaspoon paprika
- 0.5 teaspoon fennel
- 0.25 teaspoon cumin
- 2 tablespoons garlic powder
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil

## Instructions

1. Dice potatoes, peppers, and onions. Set shrimp to thaw.
2. In a large pot, heat 2 tablespoons coconut oil.
3. Once warm, add curry, garam masala, paprika, garlic, fennel, and cayenne. Stir until uniform, but not burning or popping.
4. Add chopped onions, stir, and heat until uniform and slightly translucent.
5. Add 1 can of coconut milk, stir. Check the spiciness of the curry here to see if you want more curry, garam masala, or cayenne.
6. Adjust spices and add 1 tablespoon salt, bay leaf, tomato paste, and cumin.
7. Add the second can of coconut milk, heat and stir until uniform, about 5 minutes on medium heat.
8. Boil diced potatoes in salty water separately.
9. Add green peppers, cook and stir for about 7 minutes until slightly cooked.
10. Add shrimp and black pepper, add 1 teaspoon more salt, cook and stir for 5 minutes.
11. Add spinach and peas, simmer for 10 minutes.
12. By now, if potatoes are tender, add to curry pot and cook another 6 minutes on medium-low heat.
