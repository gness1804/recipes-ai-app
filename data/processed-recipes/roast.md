# Roast

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 1 chuck roast or shoulder roast
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- Seasoned salt, to taste
- 0.5 cup water
- 1 package Lipton onion soup mix
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup water
- 2 cups water (additional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
2. Season the roast with salt, pepper, and seasoned salt.
3. Place the roast in a roaster or large pot with a lid.
4. Add 0.5 cup of water and sprinkle the onion soup mix over the roast.
5. Cover with lid and bake for about 2 hours.
6. Check the roast after about 1 hour and 20 minutes.
7. Let the roast sit after removing from oven for 5-10 minutes.
8. Remove meat from pan and make gravy from drippings.
9. Mix 2 tablespoons flour with 1 cup water and stir.
10. Heat drippings on stove until they begin to boil.
11. Slowly add the flour-water mixture to the drippings, stirring constantly.
12. Add more water (about 2 cups) until desired thickness is achieved.
13. Let it boil while stirring constantly.
14. After the flour mixture is added to drippings, stir for about 30 seconds to 1 minute.
