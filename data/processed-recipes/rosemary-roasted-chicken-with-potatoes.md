# Rosemary roasted chicken with potatoes

Rating: 7.5/10

## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 1.5 teaspoons crushed rosemary leaves
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 0.5 teaspoon coarse ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 6 bone-in chicken thighs, skin removed (about 2 lbs.)
- 1.5 lbs. small red potatoes, cut into 1-inch cubes

## Instructions

1. Mix oil, all of the spices, and salt in a large bowl.
2. Add chicken and potatoes; toss to coat well.
3. Arrange chicken and potatoes in a single layer on a foil-lined 15x10x1-inch baking pan sprayed with non-stick cooking spray.
4. Roast in a preheated 425Â°F oven for 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and potatoes are tender, turning potatoes occasionally.
