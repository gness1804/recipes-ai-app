# Red Beans and Rice

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 lb. fully cooked smoked sausage, sliced into 0.5-inch-thick rounds
- 1 onion, chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, chopped
- 3 cans (15 oz. ea.) kidney beans, with juices
- 1 cup canned low-salt chicken broth
- 1 teaspoon Creole or Cajun seasoning or 0.5 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 3 cups cooked rice

## Instructions

1. Cook the rice.
2. Heat olive oil in a heavy large Dutch oven over medium heat.
3. Add sausage, onion, and garlic and saut√© until onion is brown, about 15 minutes.
4. Mix in kidney beans with their juices, broth, and Creole seasoning.
5. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover and simmer until flavors are blended and mixture is very thick, stirring occasionally, about 45 minutes.
6. Divide cooked rice among large shallow soup bowls. Spoon bean mixture over rice and serve.
