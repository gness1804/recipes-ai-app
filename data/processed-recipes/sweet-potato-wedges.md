# Sweet potato wedges

Rating: 9/10

## Ingredients

- 1.5 to 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- Smoked paprika or garlic powder (optional)

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425Â°F.
2. Scrub the potatoes. Peel only if the skins bug you.
3. Cut each potato lengthwise into wedges. Aim for 6 to 8 wedges per potato.
4. Toss with olive oil, salt, and optional smoked paprika or garlic powder.
5. Arrange cut-side down on a baking sheet.
6. Roast for 20 minutes, flip, then roast another 10 to 15 minutes until deeply browned.
