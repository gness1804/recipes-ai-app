# Chocolate_caramel_brownies

Rating: 9/10

## Ingredients

- 1 pkg. German chocolate cake mix
- 0.75 cup melted margarine
- 1 can evaporated milk
- 6 oz. pkg. chocolate chips
- 1 bag (14 oz.) caramels, about 44

## Instructions

1. Combine cake mix, 0.33 cup of the milk, and the margarine.
2. Press half of this mixture into the bottom of a greased 13x9 pan.
3. Bake for 5 minutes at 350°F.
4. Take out and sprinkle chocolate chips over the baked crust.
5. Melt the caramels with remaining milk and spread over the chocolate chips.
6. Top with remaining cake mixture, pressing gently.
7. Bake at 350°F for about 20 minutes or until firm.
8. Cool (refrigerate for a while) and cut into squares.
