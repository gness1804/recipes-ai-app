# Tacos

Rating: 9/10

## Ingredients

- Tortillas
- 1 lb. ground meat (turkey, pork, beef, deer)
- Salsa
- 1 green pepper, cut into pieces
- 1 cup water
- 1 pack taco seasoning

## Instructions

1. Cut up green pepper and place in a bowl on the table.
2. Cook the meat until done.
3. Add taco seasoning and water.
4. Mix together and cook until most of the water cooks down.
5. Serve in tortillas with salsa and green pepper on the table.
