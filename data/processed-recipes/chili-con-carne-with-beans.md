# Chili Con Carne with Beans

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 1.5 cups dried pinto beans
- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 0.5 cup chopped onion
- 0.5 cup chopped green pepper
- 1 lb. ground beef
- 2 cans (1 lb. ea.) tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- 1 teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Wash beans; cover with water and soak overnight.
2. Cook in boiling salted water until tender; drain.
3. In hot oil, cook garlic, onion, green pepper, and ground beef until lightly browned.
4. Add remaining ingredients and simmer about 2 hours, adding water if necessary.
5. Add cooked beans and heat thoroughly.
