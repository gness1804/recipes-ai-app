# Turkey & Cabbage Rice-Noodle Stir-Fry

Rating: 4/10

## Ingredients

- 1 lb. ground turkey (93% lean)
- 0.5 medium head green cabbage, thinly sliced
- 2 carrots, cut into matchsticks
- 8 oz. wide rice noodles
- 3 to 4 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
- 1 to 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 2 inches fresh ginger, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
- 1 bunch scallions, sliced
- Chili-garlic sauce or crushed red pepper (optional, at table)

## Instructions

1. Soak or cook the noodles according to the package instructions; drain.
2. In a large pan, brown the turkey in 1 tablespoon of neutral oil over medium-high heat for 4 to 5 minutes, breaking it up as it cooks. Add the ginger (and garlic if using); cook for an additional 30 seconds.
3. Add the cabbage and carrots to the pan; stir-fry for 4 to 5 minutes until just tender.
4. Toss in the noodles, soy sauce, and rice vinegar; stir until the noodles are glossy and coated, about 1 to 2 minutes.
5. Remove from heat and fold in the scallion greens. Serve with optional chili-garlic sauce or crushed red pepper on the side.
