# Quick and Easy Vegetable Soup

Rating: N

## Ingredients

- 1 can (14 oz.) chicken broth
- 1 can (11.5 oz.) tomato-vegetable juice cocktail
- 1 cup water
- 1 large potato, diced
- 2 carrots, sliced
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 1 can (14.5 oz.) diced tomatoes
- 1 cup fresh green beans, chopped
- 1 cup fresh corn kernels
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Creole seasoning, to taste
- Okra, fresh or frozen

## Instructions

1. In a large stock pot, combine broth, tomato juice, water, potatoes, carrots, celery, undrained chopped tomatoes, green beans, and corn.
2. Season with salt, pepper, and Creole seasoning.
3. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 minutes or until all vegetables are tender.
