# Mom's Chicken Pomodoro

Rating: 7/10

## Ingredients

- 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast half
- 1 tablespoon parmesan cheese
- 1 packet (2g) Mrs. Dash Tomato Basil Garlic Seasoning
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 8 cherry tomatoes, sliced or 0.5 cup chopped tomatoes, canned
- 4 oz. penne pasta, cooked and drained
- 0.5 cup sliced fresh vegetables of your choice (optional)

## Instructions

1. Pound chicken flat.
2. In a bowl, combine parmesan cheese and Mrs. Dash Tomato Basil Garlic Seasoning.
3. Dip chicken in mixture; coat both sides evenly.
4. Heat olive oil in a small pan over medium heat; add chicken.
5. Cover and cook for 3 minutes.
6. Turn over and cook an additional 2 minutes or until no longer pink in center.
7. Cut into strips and set aside.
8. Add tomatoes and any remaining vegetables into the pan. Simmer for about 2 minutes.
9. Add cooked pasta and toss. Top with cooked chicken.
10. Sprinkle additional parmesan cheese if desired.
