# Basmati Rice with Sausage and Black Beans

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 2 cups basmati rice, cooked
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 6 strips bacon, cut into 0.5-inch pieces
- 0.5 lb. smoked pork or beef sausage, sliced into 0.25-inch rounds
- 1 can (15 oz.) black beans, do not drain
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 3 bay leaves
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt, to taste

## Instructions

1. Cook the rice however desired.
2. Heat a large pot over medium heat. Add olive oil and heat.
3. Add the bacon and sausage and saut√© until brown and crispy, about 4 minutes.
4. Add the can of black beans and cook for 1 more minute.
5. Add the chicken broth, garlic powder, bay leaves, oregano, and salt. Bring to a boil and then reduce heat to low.
6. Simmer for 15 minutes or until desired thickness.
7. Serve hot over rice.
