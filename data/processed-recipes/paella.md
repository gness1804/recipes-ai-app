# Paella

Rating: 8/10

## Ingredients

- 0.25 cup olive oil
- 2 cups frozen onion
- 2 cups vegetable stock
- 2 cups paella rice, bomba rice if possible, or Calrose or another short-grained rice
- 4 cans clams, with juice
- 0.5 lb. shrimp (optional)
- Other possible seafood: mussels, squid, etc.
- Fish, like salmon
- 4 fresh tomatoes, chopped (or 2 cans diced)
- 1 small red pepper, sliced
- 1 small green pepper, sliced
- 1 small yellow pepper, sliced
- 1 cup green peas, frozen
- 0.5 can artichoke hearts, canned
- 4 tablespoons lemon juice

### For the Spice Mix
- 2 pinches saffron threads, powdered finely (or 0.5-1 teaspoon turmeric)
- 1-2 teaspoons paprika, at least 50% sweet
- 1-2 teaspoons onion powder
- 0.5-1 teaspoon cayenne
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- Dried herbs, such as rosemary or oregano
- Salt, cumin, cloves, etc., to taste

## Instructions

1. Blend the spices in a bowl and set aside. Prep all relevant ingredients by chopping, etc.
2. Heat the olive oil in a large pot and sauté the onion for 3 minutes or until translucent. Then sauté all peppers.
3. Add the rice and sauté for a further 3 minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure the rice doesn't stick.
4. Add the vegetable stock, clam juice, and about a third of the spices. Simmer for 20 minutes or until all but a little bit of the liquid has been absorbed.
5. Stir in the peas and tomatoes.
6. Add the seafood.
7. Add artichoke hearts and lemon juice to the pan.
8. Add the rest of the spices. Mix in well.
9. Continue cooking until the rice is tender, topping up with additional hot water if needed.
10. Serve the paella with lemon wedges to garnish.
