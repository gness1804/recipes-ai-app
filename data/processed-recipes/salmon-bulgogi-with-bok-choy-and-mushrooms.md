# Salmon Bulgogi with Bok Choy and Mushrooms

Rating: 8.5/10

## Ingredients

### For the Marinade
- 1 large garlic clove, peeled
- 0.33 cup chopped green onions
- 0.25 cup soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon Chinese rice wine or dry Sherry
- 1 cube (0.75-inch) peeled fresh ginger
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon Asian sesame oil
- 0.75 teaspoon chili-garlic sauce

### For the Salmon
- 4 fillets (6 oz. ea.) center-cut skinless salmon

### For the Vegetables
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 large bok choy, cut crosswise into 0.5-inch-wide strips (about 7 cups)
- 4 oz. fresh shiitake mushrooms, stemmed, caps sliced
- 1 large garlic clove, peeled

## Instructions

### For the Marinade
1. Blend 1 garlic clove, green onions, soy sauce, rice wine, ginger, sugar, sesame oil, and chili-garlic sauce in a mini processor.
2. Arrange salmon in an 11x7x2-inch glass baking dish. Spoon marinade over the salmon. Let marinate for 5 minutes.

### For the Salmon
1. Preheat oven to 500Â°F.
2. Arrange fish, with some marinade still clinging, on a rimmed baking sheet. Transfer any marinade in the dish to a small saucepan.
3. Roast fish until just opaque in the center, about 8 minutes.
4. Bring marinade in the saucepan to a boil; set aside and reserve for glaze.

### For the Vegetables
1. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over high heat.
2. Add bok choy and mushrooms; using a garlic press, press in 1 garlic clove.
3. Stir-fry until mushrooms are tender and bok choy is wilted, about 4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

### To Serve
1. Divide vegetables among plates. Top with salmon.
2. Brush fish with glaze.
