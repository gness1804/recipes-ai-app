# Gumbo

Rating: 7/10

## Ingredients

- 3-4 cups okra, chopped
- 2 cups cabbage, chopped
- 1 can tomatoes (small can or fresh), chopped
- 0.5 medium onion, chopped
- 1-2 tablespoons butter
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- 0.25 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- Garlic, to taste
- Water, as needed

## Instructions

1. Wash okra and cut off the stems. Cut into 0.5-inch pieces.
2. Add chopped tomatoes and 2 cups chopped cabbage.
3. Simmer cabbage, tomato, and okra on medium heat. Add a little water.
4. In another pan, saut√© 0.5 medium chopped onion in 1-2 tablespoons butter.
5. Add onion and butter to the okra mixture.
6. Add salt, pepper, 0.25 teaspoon chili powder, 1 teaspoon sugar, and garlic.
7. Simmer for about 20 minutes until the okra changes color and the cabbage is tender.
