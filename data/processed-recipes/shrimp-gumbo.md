# Shrimp gumbo

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 1 cup green pepper, chopped
- 1 cup celery, chopped
- 1 cup onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 0.25 cup vegetable oil
- 2 cans (10.5 oz. ea.) ready to serve beef broth
- 1 can (14.5 oz.) tomatoes, undrained and chopped
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 0.5 teaspoon dried whole oregano
- 0.5 teaspoon dried whole thyme
- 0.5 teaspoon red pepper
- 1 bay leaf
- 1.5 lbs. small shrimp, shelled and deveined
- 1 package (10 oz.) frozen sliced okra, thawed
- Hot cooked rice

## Instructions

1. Saut√© the green pepper, celery, onion, and garlic in hot oil in a Dutch oven until tender.
2. Stir in the beef broth, tomatoes, salt, oregano, thyme, red pepper, and bay leaf; bring to a boil.
3. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Add shrimp to the gumbo and simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Stir in okra and cook an additional 5 minutes.
6. Remove bay leaf and serve over rice.
