# Classic Cuban-Style Picadillo

Rating: 6.5/10

## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 0.5 green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 lb. lean ground beef
- 6 large pitted green olives, quartered
- 0.5 cup raisins
- 1 tablespoon capers (optional)
- 1 can (8 oz.) tomato sauce
- 2 packages (1.41 oz. ea.) sazon seasoning
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- Salt, to taste
- Ripe plantains
- 1 cup rice

## Instructions

1. Cut up plantains and cook in oil. Set aside.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir garlic, onion, and green bell pepper in the hot oil until softened, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Crumble ground beef into the skillet; cook and stir until browned completely, 7 to 10 minutes.
4. Stir olives, raisins, capers, tomato sauce, sazon seasoning, cumin, sugar, and salt through the ground beef mixture.
5. Cover the skillet, reduce heat to low, and cook until the mixture is heated through, 5 to 10 minutes.
