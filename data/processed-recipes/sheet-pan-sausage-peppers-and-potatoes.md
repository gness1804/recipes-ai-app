# Sheet-Pan Sausage, Peppers, and Potatoes

Rating: 4/10

## Ingredients

- 1 lb. smoked sausage or Italian sausage, cut into rounds
- 1 lb. small potatoes, quartered
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 red onion, sliced
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 0.5 teaspoon oregano
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425°F.
2. Toss potatoes, peppers, onion, and sausage with olive oil, smoked paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread on a sheet pan. Roast for 25–30 minutes, stirring once, until potatoes are crisp and everything is browned.
4. Serve with mustard or crusty bread.
