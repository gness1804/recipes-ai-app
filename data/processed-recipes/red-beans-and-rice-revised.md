# Red beans and rice - revised

Rating: 6.5/10

## Ingredients

- 1 box Zatarain's red beans and rice, follow box instructions
- 1 avocado
- 1 pack (about 1 lb.) andouille sausage
- 1 onion
- Cheddar cheese (optional, only for Sarah)

## Instructions

1. Prepare the Zatarain's red beans and rice according to the box instructions.
2. While the rice is cooking, slice the andouille sausage and chop the onion.
3. In a skillet, cook the sausage and onion over medium heat until the sausage is browned and the onion is translucent.
4. Dice the avocado.
5. Once the rice is ready, combine it with the sausage and onion mixture.
6. Serve with diced avocado on top. Add Cheddar cheese if desired.
