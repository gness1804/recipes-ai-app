# Red Beans and Rice Burritos

Rating: 5/10

## Ingredients

- 2 avocados, cut into small slices
- 1 box red beans and rice (Zatarain's)
- 1 jar salsa
- 10 burrito-sized tortillas
- Corn (optional)
- Green peppers (optional)
- Other veggies (optional)

## Instructions

1. Start by putting together all the ingredients of the rice and beans mixture and turning on the heat.
2. Cut up avocado into small slices.
3. Prepare any other veggies and place into small bowls.
4. Cook red beans and rice according to package instructions. Make sure most of the water has been cooked down.
5. Heat up 2-3 tortillas.
6. Add red beans and rice plus all veggies to the tortillas and serve.
