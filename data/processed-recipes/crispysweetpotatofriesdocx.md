# CRISPYSWEETPOTATOFRIES

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 0.25 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
- 0.5 teaspoon cinnamon
- 3 large sweet potatoes
- Canola oil, for frying

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine salt, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and cinnamon.
2. Halve each potato lengthwise and slice into 0.25-inch sticks.
3. In a large pot, heat canola oil.
4. In batches, cook fries in oil for 5 minutes or until crisp.
5. Drain on paper towels.
6. Sprinkle the cayenne mixture over the fries.
