# MomsMashedSweetPotatoes

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 0.25 cup butter
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 sweet potatoes
- Marshmallows, a few

## Instructions

1. Bake sweet potatoes at 350°F for 40 minutes. Place aluminum foil on the rack underneath the sweet potatoes in the oven to catch any juices.
2. Check if the sweet potatoes are done by poking them with a fork. Adjust cook time as needed, increasing the temperature to 400°F if necessary.
3. Once done, cut the sweet potatoes in half.
4. Peel the sweet potatoes using your fingers.
5. Mash the sweet potatoes with a fork or masher.
6. Melt butter in the microwave for approximately 20 seconds.
7. Mix the mashed sweet potatoes with melted butter and brown sugar.
8. Place marshmallows on top of the sweet potato mixture.
