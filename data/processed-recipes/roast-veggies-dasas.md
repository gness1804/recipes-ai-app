# Roast veggies (Dasas)

Rating: 7.5/10

## Ingredients

- Assorted vegetables (e.g., red onion, bell peppers), cut
- Olive oil, for drizzling
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- Oregano, to taste
- Lemon juice, to taste
- Vinegar, to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to a high temperature.
2. Place the cut vegetables on a baking sheet.
3. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
4. Roast in the oven until the edges are browned.
5. Season with pepper, salt, and oregano.
6. Add lemon juice and vinegar.
7. Chill before serving.
