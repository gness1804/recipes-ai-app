# Simple chicken stirfry

Rating: 7/10

## Ingredients

- 1 lb. chicken thighs, cut into pieces
- 1-2 cups rice
- 1 green bell pepper, cut into pieces
- 1 red bell pepper, cut into pieces
- 1-2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
- 1 teaspoon ginger
- Sesame seeds, to taste
- Other spices (optional)

## Instructions

1. Cook the rice on the side.
2. Cut up the chicken and bell peppers.
3. Cook the chicken in a pan.
4. Stir in the cut-up bell peppers.
5. Add the teriyaki sauce.
6. Add the ginger and other spices.
7. Serve over rice.
