# Moms classic spaghetti

Rating: 9.5/10

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. ground beef
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped (about 0.5 cup)
- 1 green pepper, finely chopped
- 2 cans (15 oz. ea.) tomato sauce
- 2 cans (12 oz. ea.) tomato paste
- 1 can (7.5 oz.) pitted ripe olives, drained and sliced
- 2 envelopes (1.5 oz. ea.) Italian-style spaghetti sauce mix with mushrooms
- 3 cups water
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon oregano leaves
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 bay leaf, crumbled
- 16 oz. Italian-style spaghetti
- Grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Cook and stir ground beef, onion, and green pepper until meat is brown and onion is tender.
2. Stir in remaining ingredients except spaghetti and cheese.
3. Cover and simmer for 1.5 hours, stirring sauce occasionally.
4. Cook spaghetti as directed.
5. Serve meat sauce over hot spaghetti and pass Parmesan cheese.
