# Salmon with capers

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 1 lb. salmon
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 1 lemon, juiced
- Dijon mustard, to taste
- 1 cup quinoa

## Instructions

1. Defrost salmon.
2. Place salmon in a baking dish.
3. Cover salmon with capers, lemon juice, and Dijon mustard (used sparingly).
4. Cook fish for about 15 minutes at 450Â°F.
5. Cook quinoa according to package directions; serve under salmon.
