# spaghetti w clam sauce

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 0.5 cup chopped onion
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 0.25 teaspoon basil
- 0.25 teaspoon salt
- 1 bay leaf
- 0.25 teaspoon pepper
- 2 teaspoons brown sugar
- 1 can (28 oz.) diced tomatoes
- 1 can (6 oz.) tomato paste
- 4 teaspoons parsley
- 2 cans (6.5 oz. each) minced clams, liquid drained
- Cooked spaghetti noodles

## Instructions

1. Saut√© onion and garlic in olive oil. Then add bay leaf, basil, salt, pepper, brown sugar, tomatoes, tomato paste, parsley, and liquid drained from canned clams.
2. Simmer uncovered for 30 minutes to 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Mix in clams.
3. Serve over cooked spaghetti noodles.
