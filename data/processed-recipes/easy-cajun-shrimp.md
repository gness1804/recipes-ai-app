# Easy Cajun Shrimp

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 1 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined, tail off
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
- Kosher salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- Lime wedges, for garnish
- Fresh parsley or cilantro, for garnish
- Cooked jasmine rice or Cajun rice

## Instructions

1. Toss shrimp with Cajun seasoning, salt, and pepper.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp and cook 2-3 minutes per side, until pink and opaque.
3. Serve over rice or cauliflower rice. Garnish with lime and parsley or cilantro.
