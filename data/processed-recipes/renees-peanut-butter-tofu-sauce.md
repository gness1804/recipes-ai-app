# Renee's Peanut Butter Tofu Sauce

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 4 to 5 tablespoons peanut butter
- 1.5 tablespoons coconut oil
- 1 teaspoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon sriracha
- Pinch of basil/garam masala
- Garlic salt, to taste
- Cumin, to taste
- 2 shakes of chili powder
- Cilantro, to taste
- 1 teaspoon almond milk

## Instructions

1. Add and heat oil in a pan.
2. Add spices, except for sriracha, and stir to heat.
3. Add peanut butter and stir lightly. Ensure it does not burn.
4. Add vinegar and heat.
5. Add almond milk and mix.
6. Check consistency and spices. Stir until uniform and warm.
