# Coconut-Ginger Salmon with Green Beans (over Jasmine Rice)

Rating: N

## Ingredients

- 1.25 lb. salmon fillets (4 portions)
- 1 can (13.5 oz.) light coconut milk
- 2–3 inches fresh ginger, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
- 12 oz. green beans, trimmed
- 1 bunch scallions, sliced (whites and greens separated)
- 1.5 cups jasmine rice, uncooked
- 2–3 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce or tamari
- 0.5 teaspoon ground turmeric
- Lime wedges (optional, serve on the side)

## Instructions

1. Rinse 1.5 cups jasmine rice and cook according to package instructions.
2. Warm 1 tablespoon neutral oil in a pan. Sauté scallion whites and ginger (and garlic if using) for 1–2 minutes. Stir in turmeric, then add coconut milk and soy sauce. Simmer for 3–4 minutes.
3. Add salmon (skin side down if skin-on) and nestle in green beans. Cover and simmer gently for 8–10 minutes until salmon flakes and beans are crisp-tender.
4. Taste and adjust soy sauce as needed. Serve over rice and top with scallion greens. Serve lime wedges on the side for anyone who wants it.
