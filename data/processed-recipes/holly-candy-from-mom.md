# Holly candy (from Mom)

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 35 large marshmallows
- 1 stick butter
- 4.5 cups cornflakes
- 2 teaspoons green food coloring
- 1 bag red hots

## Instructions

1. Melt marshmallows and butter together.
2. Add food coloring and stir (45 strokes).
3. Add cornflakes and stir until flakes are dark green.
4. Drop on wax paper.
5. Place 3 red hots in the middle of each.
