# shrimp creole (mom version)

Rating: 7/10

## Ingredients

- 1 lb. cooked, cleaned shrimp
- 1 large can tomatoes
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 3 stalks celery, diced
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 teaspoons chili powder
- 1 cup cooked green peas
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 6 cups wild rice, cooked
- 1 drop Tabasco
- 0.25 cup white wine

## Instructions

1. Chop celery and saut√© with onion in olive oil until transparent.
2. Mix in chili powder and flour thoroughly.
3. Add tomatoes and bring to a simmer; add sugar, Tabasco, and white wine.
4. Simmer until thickened, about 15-20 minutes.
5. Add shrimp and cook only until they are heated through.
6. Serve at once on a bed of hot wild rice.
