# Mango Salad

Rating: N

## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 0.5 teaspoon salt
- 0.25 teaspoon black pepper
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 lb. firm-ripe mango, peeled and cut into 0.5-inch cubes
- 1 large tomato, cut into 0.5-inch cubes
- 1 small red onion, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced crosswise
- 0.33 cup fresh cilantro leaves

## Instructions

1. Whisk together vinegar, lemon juice, sugar, salt, and pepper until sugar is dissolved.
2. Add oil, whisking until emulsified.
3. Add remaining ingredients and toss until coated.
