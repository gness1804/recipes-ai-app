# Cuban Chicken Stew with Cinnamon

Rating: [MISSING]

## Ingredients

- 3 lbs. chicken, cut into pieces
- 8 oz. ham, diced
- 0.5 cup sofrito broth
- 1.5 cups tomato, diced
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- 3 tablespoons oil
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- Adobo seasoning powder, to taste
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Season the chicken with the adobo seasoning powder. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to an hour.
2. Heat oil in a skillet and saut√© the sofrito and ham over medium heat for 5 minutes.
3. Add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, and water, and bring to a boil.
4. Add chicken, cinnamon, salt, and pepper, and bring to a boil again.
5. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes.
